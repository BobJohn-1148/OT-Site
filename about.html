About the clinic

Family-centered pediatric rehabilitation

Evergreen Occupational Therapy is a pediatric OT clinic committed to serving children with individualized care. We believe progress happens when caregivers, therapists, and teachers work together.

  • Mission: help children build independence, confidence, and functional skills.
  • Values: collaboration, compassion, and evidence-based practice.
  • Delivery options: in-person, telehealth, and (when available) school-based services.
