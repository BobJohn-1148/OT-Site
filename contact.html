Contact & Booking

How to get started, what happens next, and clinic policies.

Booking steps

  1. Request an appointment: complete our online request form and a provider will call you back.
  2. Initial consultation: discuss concerns and treatment options; we create a care plan.
  3. Insurance and payment: we accept multiple insurances and offer private pay options.
  4. Visit preparation & policies: review policies before your first visit.

Policy note: We have a 24-hour cancellation fee of $25. Please contact the office about scheduling conflicts.
